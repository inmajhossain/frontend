FAQ

Here you can find the answers to most frequently asked questions.

Booking

How do I book a chalet?

Booking a chalet with Swiss Chalets is easy! Simply navigate to the chalet of your choice and select your desired dates. Follow the prompts to complete your booking securely online.

Is there a minimum stay requirement?

The minimum stay requirement varies depending on the chalet and the time of year. Please refer to the chalet's listing page for specific details.

Can I make changes to my booking?

Yes, you can make changes to your booking depending on availability. Contact our customer support team for assistance with modifications.

Amenities

What amenities are included in the chalet?

Our chalets are equipped with a range of amenities, including fully furnished interiors, modern kitchen appliances, cozy fireplaces, complimentary Wi-Fi, and more. Please refer to the chalet's listing page for a detailed list of amenities.

Are linens and towels provided?

Yes, all linens and towels are provided for your convenience.

Payment

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express, as well as bank transfers for certain bookings.

When is payment due?

Payment is typically due at the time of booking. However, for longer stays or special arrangements, payment schedules may vary. Please refer to your booking confirmation for details.

Cancellation policy

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the chalet and the time of year. Please refer to the chalet's listing page or your booking confirmation for specific details.

What happens if I need to cancel my reservation?

If you need to cancel your reservation, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. Refund eligibility will depend on the terms of our cancellation policy and the timing of your cancellation.

General

Are pets allowed in the chalets?

Pets are not allowed in our chalets, with the exception of service animals.

Is smoking allowed in the chalets?

Smoking is strictly prohibited inside our chalets. However, designated outdoor smoking areas may be available.

Do you offer discounts for longer stays?

Yes, we offer discounts for longer stays. Please contact our customer support team for more information.
